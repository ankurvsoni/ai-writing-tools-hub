### Live product snapshot (US Amazon, at time of writing)
- ErGear Electric Standing Desk 47x24 — $94.99 • 4.5 out of 5 • (4,384) • https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B41YH9B6/?tag=triedandtr0d5-20
- ErGear Electric Standing Desk 48x24 — $129.99 • 4.4 out of 5 • (1,296) • https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B4218ZG3/?tag=triedandtr0d5-20
- ErGear Electric Standing Desk 55x28 — $169.98 • 4.5 out of 5 • (4,384) • https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B41Z34M7/?tag=triedandtr0d5-20
- FLEXISPOT EN2 Standing Desk — $229.99 • 4.5 out of 5 • (4,691) • https://www.amazon.com/dp/B07W8W8XKV/?tag=triedandtr0d5-20


### Quick verdict
If your back gets stiff after lunch, a standing desk is worth it. But only if the frame is stable at your height and the desktop is deep enough for your monitor distance. For most solopreneurs, 48"x24" is the minimum practical size.

### Comparison table
| Pick type | Best for | Tradeoff |
|---|---|---|
| Budget electric desk | First upgrade | More wobble at max height |
| Mid-range dual-motor | Daily heavy use | Costs more up front |
| Premium frame + top | Long-term build | Higher setup effort |

### Top picks with pros/cons
- Budget electric desk: good entry, memory presets, usually fastest ROI. Con: narrower tops, thinner steel.
- Mid-range dual motor: quieter lift, better stability. Con: heavier and pricier.
- Frame-only premium build: best long-term feel. Con: sourcing top and assembly time.

### Who should skip this
Skip for now if you still work from a kitchen chair. Chair and monitor height fix pain faster per dollar.

### Common mistakes
Buying by width only, ignoring depth. Ignoring cable slack for sit/stand movement. Choosing a desktop that chips around clamp mounts.

### Budget tiers
- Under $250: basic electric frame + simple top.
- $250–$500: dual motor + better top.
- $500+: premium frame, cable tray, anti-fatigue mat.

### FAQ
- Is 24" depth enough? Bare minimum for one monitor, better at 27–30".
- Do I need dual motors? Not always, but stability improves.

### Final recommendation
Start with a stable 48x24 electric desk and one cable tray. Add extras later.
